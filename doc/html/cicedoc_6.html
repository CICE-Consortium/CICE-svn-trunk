Mechanical redistribution

The last term on the right-hand side of the ITD equation describes the redistribution of ice in thickness space due to ridging and other mechanical processes. The mechanical redistribution scheme in CICE is based on [7], [13], [14], and [15]. This scheme converts thinner ice to thicker ice and is applied after horizontal transport. When the ice is converging, enough ice ridges to ensure that the ice area does not exceed the grid cell area. For details, see the PostScript documentation.
